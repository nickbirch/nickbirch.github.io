About Me

I believe in a future where our workforce is able to keep up with the continually evolving skills needed to be successful, without incurring significant debt. I'm a co-founder of Eleven Fifty Academy and PropelUp, both of which are focused on enabling this future. Now, I am taking the Web Development class at Eleven Fifty Academy to learn how to write code, myself, so that I can help build our application and understand the challenges of learning technology skills.

I'm a speaker on entrepreneurship and technology and my writing has been featured in the Indianapolis Business Journal, VentureBeat, and TechPoint. In 2017, 2018, and 2019, I was a nominee for TechPoint's Rising Entrepreneur/Star award. In 2019, I was a Mitch Daniels Leadership Fellow. I also volunteer hundreds of hours a year as a baseball coach for North Central High School and for JobSpark, a Junior Achievement event for Indianapolis 8th graders.
